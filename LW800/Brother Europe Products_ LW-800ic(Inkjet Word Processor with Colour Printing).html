Overview

Brother's line of Inkjet Word Processors include portable models and a desktop model. The other models in the line include LW-810ic/810icBL,LW-830ic(desktop) and LW-840ic. All of the Inkjet Word Processors include a 1.44MB floppy disk drive, straight paper path with a 30 page sheet feeder, Perfectype(r) Professional Touch Keyboard and ink jet printer with colour printing capability. See the product specification pages for details on the features of each model.

The LW-800ic allows you to create stylised reports, presentations, newsletters and brochures by combining multiple type styles, fonts, colours and sizes into a single document. There are over 2000 different print variations. In addition, you can add clip art or special symbols and images to your documents.
