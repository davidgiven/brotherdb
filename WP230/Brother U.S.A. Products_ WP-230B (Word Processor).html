Create professional-looking documents by combining daisy wheel printing technology with easy-to-use word processing software. The 14 line display and 1.44MB disk drive make word processing easier than ever before. The BOOKMAN(R) slot on the bottom of the unit provides access to a variety of reference titles. More than 30 BOOKMAN(R) cards are now available, with many more to come.

OVERVIEW

Brother's line of daisywheel word processors include both portable and desktop models. The portable models, the WP-1700MDS and WP-230B, include 14 line LCD displays, 1.44MB disk drives, Perfectype(R) professional touch keyboads and built-in daisy wheel printers. The other daisy wheel models, WP-330MDS and WP-335B, are desktop models containing a base unit and separate monitor

The WP-230B is suited to customers looking for full featured word processing plus portability. This 13 lb. model, with handle, can be left on a desk or easily stored away when not in use. The 1.44MB disk drive provides unlimited memory storage capability. Plus, the files can be converted to most major PC word processing packages if you need to share files with a PC.
