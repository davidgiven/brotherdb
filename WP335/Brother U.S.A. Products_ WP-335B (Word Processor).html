Create professional-looking documents by combining daisy wheel printing technology with easy-to-use word processing software. The 14" amber CRT and 1.44MB disk drive make word processing easier than ever before. The BOOKMAN(R) slot on the bottom of the unit provides access to a variety of reference titles. More than 30 BOOKMAN(R) cards are now available, with many more to come.

OVERVIEW

Brother's line of daisywheel word processors include desktop and portable models. The desktop models, WP-335B and WP-330MDS, contain base units with separate monitors, 1.44MB disk drives, Perfectype(R) professional touch keyboards and built-in daisy wheel printers. The portable models, WP-1700MDS and WP-230B, include 14 line LCD displays.

The WP-335B is well suited to customers looking for a full featured word processor that is easy to use and produces letter quality professional documents. The 14" monitor makes creating and editing simple. The 1.44MB disk drive provides unlimited memory storage capability. Plus, the files can be converted to most major PC word processing packages if you need to share files with a PC.
