Overview

A total design and document creation centre that's easy to use. When you write in colour, it gets seen, it gets reads and it definitely gets remembered. Colour gives the written word the kind of impact that gets results. For even more dramatic effects, the Brother LW-750ic joins colour with design, graphics, clip art, chart making, plus word processing capabilities to help you create the most professional-looking documents.

By combining a 14" colour monitor, colour inkjet printer, mouse, Professional Touch Keyboard, and a suite of award-winning software, the LW-750ic lets you be more creative than ever before. The LW-750ic is packed full of time saving features. The LW-750ic includes both colour and black ink cartridges along with a disk containing 31 colour clip art images.

Thanks to the economy and high performance of its GEOS(R) operating system, the LW-750ic helps you do it all at a price almost anyone can afford.


Features Include

  • 14" Colour high resolution graphic monitor
  • Laser quality 300 x 300 dpi color inkjet printer
  • 3.5" 1.44MB floppy disk drive
  • Import/Export word processing and spreadsheet files to major PC software packages such as LOTUS(R) 1-2-3(R), MS Word(R), and Word Perfect(R)
  • Point and Click Mouse
  • Professional touch keyboard
  • Suite of Award Winning Software

Specifications

  • 12" carriage width, 9.1" printing width
  • 300 x 300 dpi printing
  • 640 x 400 screen resolution
  • Up to 160 characters per second printing speed (monochrome printing)
  • Maximum file size - 100K per file
  • 1.44MB or 720KB 3.5" disks
  • IN-700 Black Ink Cartridge (1,000,000 Char. Yield 12 pt.)
  • IN-710C Colour Ink Cartridge (200 page yield with 15% coverage)
  • Includes Colour and Black Ink Cartridges
  • Unit dimensions: 455mm(17.9") x 148mm(5.8") x 410mm(16.1")
  • Unit weight: 5kg
  • CRT dimensions: 368mm(14.4") x 370mm(14.6") x 367mm(14.4")
Optional Accessories

  • Hand Held Image Scanner (SC-700)
  • Cut Sheet Feeder (CF-710)
  • Black Ink Cartridge (IN-700)
  • Colour Ink Cartridge - Tri-color System (IN-710C)
  • Black Ink Refilling System (IN-700RF)
  • Colour Refilling System (IN-710CRF)
